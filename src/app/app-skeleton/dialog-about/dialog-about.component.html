About this Data

Coronavirus Weekly Positive Cases in England:

The Map shows a seven day rolling total for the number of positive cases in local areas across England. Cases are reported by Middle Layer Super Output Areas (MSOA) - which are a standard statistical geography of approximately 7,200 people.

Data Source:

Public Health England Second Generation Surveillance System (SGSS). Dates are the final day in the relevant 7-day block, and small numbers between 0 and 2 are hidden in the data to protect confidentiality.

For more details go to https://coronavirus.data.gov.uk/

Note: Map data is manually maintained and therefore may not represent the latest figures from Public Health England.
